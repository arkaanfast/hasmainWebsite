What We Do

General Contracting

The General Contracting business is supported by our in-house design team and our experienced Project Management team. This helps us keep a clear sight of our vision of being the leading General contractor in the South India. Our key Service areas include –

  • - EPC Solutions
  • - Turnkey Solution
  • - Project Management

Real Estate Development

UK Buildcon Pvt. Ltd. aims to be a premier Real Estate developer in Karnataka, developing high quality homes at affordable prices. Our Real Estate developments will be done in a Joint Venture along with Hasmai Construction.

Interior Design

We do end-to-end interior design and fitouts via UK Buildcon Fitout Works. It is the latest division of the UK Buildcon Construction Group. UK Buildcon Fitouts offers a wide range of services such as –

  • – Architectural and Interior Design
  • – Coordinating with Consultants
  • – Cost Estimation
  • – Interior Fit-out & Fitments

Faculty Management

UK Buildcon Pvt. Ltd. is the region’s most integrated Facility Management Company. We offer a whole host of specialized solutions as well as core Facility Management services such as MEP, HVAC Systems Maintenance, Civil Works and Infrastructure Maintenance. We are a true one-stop shop for managing any building, community or commercial park.
